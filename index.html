📝 Version History

v5.2 · reviewed core list & LTO handling
  • Core designations now come from the reviewed SKU master list (165 core across all lines) instead of automatic thresholds. Opportunity is driven by missing core items only.
  • LTO items are excluded from opportunity — limited-time SKUs are tagged LTO and never counted as a distribution gap (currently 2 on Cafe Valley).
  • Rich's opportunity now differs by bakery tier. A store only accrues opportunity on core items its tier can make/use: Full Bakery gets cakes & icings; Basic Bakery adds frozen dough & cookies; Fully Finished Only is limited to finished/deli/parfait categories. Same core list, tier-eligible only.
  • Full-line redefined as carrying a target share of a store's tier-eligible core (100% was unreachable with the larger core set). Bar is 80% for most lines; Rich's uses 30% since its core set is broad and network-wide coverage tops out near 40%. Store profiles show core coverage %.
  • CORE and LTO tags surface on the SKU line and store SKU tiles.
v5.1 · volume-adjusted opportunity
  • Opportunity is now volume-adjusted per store. Each store's missing-SKU opportunity scales by its own 4-week revenue vs. the vendor's median carrying store (clamped 0.25×–3.0×), so a small account no longer shows the same per-SKU gap as a high-volume one.
  • Every opportunity figure is shown as two numbers: per week and annual (weekly × 52) — on the store profile, per-SKU tiles, client scorecards, and portfolio dashboard.
  • Corrected the annual distribution-gap math (previously derived from a 4-week internal figure); weekly and annual are now computed from a single volume-adjusted weekly base.
